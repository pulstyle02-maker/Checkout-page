Epithelial Tissue Architecture

Characterized by close cell apposition and presence at a free surface. Avascular structure relying on diffusion from underlying lamina propria.

Simple Squamous

  • Endothelium (blood vessels)
  • Mesothelium (body cavities)
  • Bowman's capsule (kidney)

Clinical Correlate

Metaplasia occurs when one mature epithelial type is replaced by another (e.g., Barrett's esophagus: squamous to columnar).
